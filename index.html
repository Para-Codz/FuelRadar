Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about FuelRadar, fuel prices, and community updates.

How does FuelRadar get fuel prices?

FuelRadar relies on drivers like you to share real-time fuel prices at stations. The community updates the data instantly.

All updates are timestamped, so you always see the most recent prices. You can also filter by fuel type and city.

Can I earn tips for sharing updates?

Yes! Helpful updates can earn small tips from the community as a way to appreciate your contribution.

Tipping is currently a simple "thank you" system, but real monetary tips are coming soon.

Is FuelRadar available in my city?

Coverage is growing. You can check your city in the app or website. Your updates help expand coverage faster!

How accurate are the fuel prices?

Multiple drivers reporting similar prices increases reliability. Each update shows the time and contributor for transparency.

Can I edit or delete my updates?

Currently, updates cannot be edited or deleted to maintain transparency. Only new updates can be added.
